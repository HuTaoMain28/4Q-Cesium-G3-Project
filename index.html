Eclipse of the Hollowed King

In an age long past, the world was ruled by the great kingdoms of man, beast, and magic. Yet, a new shadow looms over the realms, for the Hollowed King, long thought to be a myth, has stirred once more. His cursed kingdom rises from the abyss, and with it, the echoes of a forgotten war.
