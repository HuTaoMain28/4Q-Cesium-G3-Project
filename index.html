Eclipse of the Hollowed King

In an age long past, the world was ruled by the great kingdoms of man, beast, and magic. Now, their remnants lie scattered across the land, swallowed by the sands of time and the creeping grasp of corruption. Yet, a new shadow looms over the realms, for the Hollowed King, long thought to be a myth, has stirred once more. His cursed kingdom rises from the abyss, and with it, the echoes of a forgotten war.

ROLES OF THE BRAVE

The fate of the world lies in the hands of those who dare to challenge the Hollowed King. Choose your role wisely, for each has a part to play in the battle against darkness.

The Way of Hostility

In the dark lands of Cesiara, those who dare enter the Hollowed King's realm must face powerful beasts in battle. When the fight begins, the warrior may choose to strike, heal, or flee. Each strike is guided by chance — sometimes strong, sometimes weak. Healing is not always certain, but it may save thy life. Should thee choose to run, the battle shall start again, as if it never happened. Some bosses are not only strong, but clever too — they can heal themselves in the middle of the fight. Only the brave, and a bit of luck, can win the day.
